A weeekend on the seine

I was lucky enough to spend three days in Paris this month! I went with my friend and we were able to see many things while we were there

    Day1

    On our first day, we started by visiting Montmartre right away. Our second stop was the Eiffel Tower (check out that beautiful picture!) We ate dinner at a cute little bistro overlooking la seine.

    Day2

    On our second day, we went shopping on Avenue des Champss Elysees. Every single person we saw dressed in height of fashion.
    After visited some really beatiful places! We went to Notre Dame. We went on a macaron tour of paris and ate all of the best marcarons the city had to offer. After we finished shopping, we visited le Louvre.

    Day3

    The picture below is of me visiting the Arc de Triompe. It's huge! We also went to le Musee D'Orsay. Seeing the work of Renoir, Monet, And Van Gogh in person was completely breathtaking. I was surprised to see that the museum also had man sculptures including thoses by Augeste Rodin, Paul Gauguin, and Alfred Barye.
